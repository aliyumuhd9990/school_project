About Us

KBT-Marketplace was established to bridge the gap between farmers, suppliers, and consumers in Kumbotso and beyond. With a focus on the agricultural sector, we provide a user-friendly platform where local producers can showcase their products, reach a broader market, and maximize their earnings. We are committed to enhancing food security, promoting agribusiness, and leveraging technology to support sustainable farming.
