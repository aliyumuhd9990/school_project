Introduction on
KBT-Marketplace

Welcome to KBT-Marketplace, your trusted online platform for agricultural trade in Kumbotso LGA, Kano State. We connect farmers, wholesalers, and buyers, providing a seamless digital marketplace for agricultural products and services.

Our goal is to revolutionize the way agricultural goods are bought and sold, ensuring fair pricing, efficiency, and accessibility for all stakeholders.

Learn more about us

Life at KBT-Marketplace

KBT-Marketplace is a digital platform designed to facilitate the buying and selling of agricultural products in Kumbotso LGA, Kano State. The platform operates by connecting three key actors: users, farmers, and administrators, each playing a vital role in ensuring smooth transactions and marketplace efficiency.

The journey begins with user registration and authentication. Anyone interested in purchasing agricultural products can sign up as a user, while farmers who wish to sell must also create an account. However, to maintain quality and credibility, farmers are required to provide verification details, which the admin reviews before granting full access to seller privileges. This verification process ensures that only genuine farmers can list products on the marketplace.
